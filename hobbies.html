My Hobbies

This is about some of my favorite hobbies and the things I enjoy doing in my free time. These activities help me relax, express myself, and have fun, and they show a little bit of who I am and what I’m interested in.

Collecting Cologne

I’ve been collecting cologne for about three years, and it has grown into a hobby I really enjoy. I like smelling different scents. Some fragrances are fresh and clean, while others are warm, and I’ve learned how each type fits different seasons or occasions. Over time, my collection has become something I’m proud of because it shows how my taste has developed.

My favorite cologne

My favorite cologne has to be Argos Birth of Venus because it smells fresh and clean, with a bright ocean‑inspired scent. It feels unique and high‑quality, and it stands out without being too strong.

Rainbow Six Siege

One of my hobbies is playing Rainbow Six Siege. I used to play it a lot, and at one point I even hit Diamond . I haven’t really played in a while, though, so I’m probably a bit rusty now. Still, it’s one of my favorite games

My favorite Operator

“Warden is my favorite operator in Rainbow Six Siege because I like his mix of style and strategy. His glasses let him see through smoke, which makes him great for clutch plays, and his whole secret‑agent vibe is just cool.”

UFC

I enjoy watching the UFC because the sport is exciting and full of skill, and my favorite fighter is Khabib because of his discipline, focus, and the respectful way he approached every fight.

Countries I Want To Visit

These are the top 3 countries that i want to vist hopefully in 7 years or so.

  • Mexico

    Mexico is a country full of vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and unforgettable food. One of its standout cities is Mérida, known for its colorful colonial streets, lively plazas, and deep Mayan heritage. I’m drawn to Mérida for its warm atmosphere, incredible local dishes, and easy access to cenotes and ancient ruins. It’s a place that feels both peaceful and full of life, making it an irresistible destination.

  • France

    France is a country filled with art, history, and beautiful scenery, from quiet villages to sparkling coastlines. One of its standout cities is Lyon, known for its charming old town and incredible food culture. I’m drawn to Lyon for its warm atmosphere, riverside views, and the way every street feels full of character. It’s a place that invites you to explore slowly and enjoy every moment.

  • Japan

    Japan is a country where ancient tradition meets modern energy, creating a culture that feels both peaceful and exciting. Kyoto stands out as one of its most beautiful cities, known for quiet temples, historic wooden streets, and stunning seasonal scenery. I’m drawn to Kyoto for its calm atmosphere and the sense of history in every corner.

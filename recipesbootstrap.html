Birria Tacos

Birria is a traditional Mexican stew from Jalisco, made from marinated meat slow‑cooked in a rich broth with chiles, garlic, and spices.

  • Ingredients
  • Beef chuck roast, short ribs, beef shank, lamb shoulder, or goat.
  • Dried chiles: Guajillo, Ancho, Arbol.
  • Aromatics: Onion, garlic, tomatoes.
  • Spices: Oregano, cumin, cinnamon, cloves, peppercorns, bay leaves, ginger.
  • Liquids: Beef broth, water, vinegar.

  • Instructions
  • Prep your ingredients: slice onions, quarter tomatoes, crush garlic, and clean the chiles.
  • Toast the spices in a dry skillet for 5 minutes.
  • Sear the beef in oil until browned.
  • Build the flavor base: sauté garlic, onions, tomatoes, then add spices and chiles.
  • Blend and strain the sauce until smooth.
  • Pressure cook the beef with the sauce for about 72 minutes.
  • Shred the meat and simmer it in extra sauce.
  • Dip tortillas in the sauce and fry with mozzarella until crispy.
  • Assemble with meat, onions, cilantro, and serve with consommé.
